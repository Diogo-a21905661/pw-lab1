Poema sobre Seoul


O My Hearts

by Chong Hyon-jong

This day is so fair
dusk is in its own hue
sky in its own hue
clouds in their own hue
and these are the cumulus clouds
that I used to see as a child
O my hearts
twilight-heart
sky-heart
cloud-heart
O heart of its own hue
